Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 3, 2024

1. Data Collection

MapSnap.ai collects the following data:

  • Google Authentication Data: Google ID, name, and email address are collected to manage user subscriptions and prevent excessive usage.
  • Travel Itineraries: The itineraries provided by users are sent to the MapSnap.ai server to extract locations with the assistance of the OpenAI API.

2. Data Usage

  • Google Authentication Data: This information is used solely to identify users and manage subscription status. It is not shared or sold.
  • Itinerary Data: Itineraries are processed by being sent to OpenAI via MapSnap.ai servers to extract relevant locations. This data is not stored on our servers.

3. Data Sharing

  • OpenAI: Itinerary data is sent to OpenAI for processing, subject to their terms and policies.
  • No Other Third Parties: User data is not shared with any other third parties. In the future, we may integrate with analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics), but user data will remain private.

4. Data Security

All data transmissions between your browser, our servers, and third-party services are encrypted using HTTPS to ensure your information is secure.

5. User Rights

Currently, users cannot delete the stored Google Authentication Data directly. However, if this is critical, users may contact the developer at mapsnap.ai@gmail.com to request that their authentication data be manually deleted from our servers. We will update our policy if an automated deletion capability is added in the future.

6. Use of Third-Party Services

MapSnap.ai utilizes the OpenAI API to process travel itineraries. The data sent to OpenAI is handled according to their privacy policies.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on our website with the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy, please contact us at mapsnap.ai@gmail.com
